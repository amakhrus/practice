About the site

Clear, independent test prep for learners who need a practical path forward.

Independent Test Prep LMS is a free learning site for GED® and SAT® preparation. It brings lessons, worked examples, diagrams, diagnostic guidance, and practice questions into one place so students can study with structure instead of guessing what to do next.

Browse courses Take the diagnostic

Why this site exists

Many learners preparing for high-stakes exams are not starting from the same place. Some are returning to school after years away. Some are balancing work, family, and study time. Some know the content but need practice reading test-style questions. Others need the basics rebuilt patiently, one topic at a time.

This site is designed for that reality. The goal is to make study feel organized, honest, and doable: find the right starting point, learn the skill, practice it, and move forward with a little more confidence.

What learners can do here

Study by topic

Courses are grouped by program, subject, and topic family so learners can jump directly to math foundations, algebra, geometry, science, language arts, social studies, or SAT® review.

Learn step by step

Lessons use plain language, worked examples, and diagrams where they help. The emphasis is understanding the method, not memorizing a trick.

Practice with feedback

Practice questions include explanations so learners can see why an answer works and where common mistakes usually happen.

Find a starting point

The diagnostic quiz helps learners identify stronger and weaker areas, then points them toward courses that match what they need to review.

How the content is created

All lessons, examples, and practice questions are independently created for study purposes. The content is written to reflect the skills learners commonly need for GED® and SAT® preparation, while avoiding copied official test questions or proprietary exam material.

  • Lessons focus on practical explanations and repeatable problem-solving routines.
  • Math courses are organized into smaller topic paths so students do not have to scroll through one long catalog.
  • Questions are built for practice and learning, with explanations that show the reasoning behind the answer.
  • The site is meant to keep improving over time as more courses, questions, and learner-support features are added.

Who it is for

Independent Test Prep LMS is useful for adult learners, high school students, tutors, parents, guardians, and adult education programs looking for a straightforward study resource. Learners can start without an account, browse lessons freely, and use practice tools to build momentum.

For younger learners, the site should be used with appropriate parent, guardian, school, or responsible adult permission. More detail is available on the Parents & Minors page.

Our learning values

Clarity over clutter

Pages are kept focused so learners can find the lesson or practice set they need without sorting through unnecessary noise.

Confidence through practice

Progress comes from repeated, understandable practice. The site supports that rhythm with lessons, questions, and explanations in the same course path.

Respect for learners

The site is free to begin, avoids ads, and is built around useful study support rather than pressure or promises.

Independent and transparent

Independent Test Prep LMS is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by GED Testing Service LLC, the American Council on Education, College Board, or any official testing organization. GED® and SAT® names are used only to identify the exams learners may be preparing for.
